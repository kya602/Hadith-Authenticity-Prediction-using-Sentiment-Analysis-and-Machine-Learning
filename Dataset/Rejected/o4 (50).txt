Narrated Abu Ahmed, Hatem Ben Siah Marwazi told us Abdul Razak, told us Muammar, the son of Ajlan, Yazid bin Ksat, from Abu Salamah, from Abu Hurayrah, said we call on Jafar ibn Abi Talib, may Allah be pleased with him the father of the poor, we used if We gave us closer to him what He came, and we came to him one day, and found him nothing, and took out a jar of honey, and he broke it; My father was Salama from my father Huraira.
