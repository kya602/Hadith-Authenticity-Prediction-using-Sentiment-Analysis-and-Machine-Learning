Narrated Abu Musa, Muhammad bin Muthanna Ibrahim bin Yacoub and one said to us Hamad bin Isa Juhani, Hanzalah ibn Abi Sufyan Jamhi from Salim bin Abdullah, from his father, Omar ibn al-Khattab, may Allah be pleased with him, said the Messenger of Allah, may Allah bless him And if he raises his hands in the du'aa ', he will not take them away, until he wipes them with his face.
