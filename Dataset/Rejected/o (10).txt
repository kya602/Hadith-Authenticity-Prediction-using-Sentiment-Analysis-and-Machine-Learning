Tell us Amr ibn Ali, said: I heard Yahya, said to us Ali bin Mubarak, said Yahya ibn Abi many, Umar ibn Mattab, that Abu Hassan, the freed slave of Bani Nofal told him said, I and my wife, owned Vtalegtha Ttliqtin then Oatguena all asked Ibn 'Abbas said, If you revise it, you have had one that the Messenger of Allaah (peace and blessings of Allaah be upon him) did so.
