Tell us Mohammed bin Muthanna, and son, Bashar both for Gander, the son of Muthanna said to us Mohammed bin Jaafar, told of the Division, said I heard Qatada, going from Anas, said, I prayed with the Messenger of Allah, peace be upon him and Abu Bakr, Omar and Othman did not hear any of them read (In the name of God the Merciful).
